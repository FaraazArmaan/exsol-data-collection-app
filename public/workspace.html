Exports

Every export downloads as a ZIP containing the catalog file (XLSX or CSV) plus a manifest.json describing the filter, row count, and generation timestamp. Filters above (search / status / marketplace) apply; sync path supports up to 500 products at a time — narrow with filters if you exceed that.

Backups

A backup is a ZIP snapshot of this workspace: all products, marketplace overlays, stock ledger, audit events, and image bytes, plus a manifest.json. Designed for disaster recovery; keep at least a recent copy off-platform.
